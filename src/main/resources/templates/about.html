About Us

Smart Content Manager is a powerful content management system designed to help users create, manage, and publish digital content with ease. Whether you're a small business or an enterprise, our platform provides you with tools for seamless collaboration, publishing, and authentication through Google and GitHub.

Built on the Spring Boot framework, Smart Content Manager offers a robust backend with Thymeleaf templating for dynamic content rendering. We are committed to providing a secure and scalable solution that helps users streamline their content creation process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations by providing them with a simple yet powerful tool to manage their content. With built-in authentication and flexible content handling, we aim to make content management easier and more efficient.
